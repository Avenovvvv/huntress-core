THE UPA ERA

The Congress-led UPA government focused heavily on welfare, institutional democracy and economic liberalization.

RTI, MGNREGA and social welfare schemes became major policies.

However, corruption scandals, policy paralysis and dynastic politics weakened public trust.

THE NDA ERA

Under Narendra Modi, India saw rapid infrastructure expansion, digital modernization, highway growth, railway upgrades and global political visibility.

UPI, Digital India and centralized governance became defining features.

Critics argue that polarization, media pressure and democratic concerns also increased.

FINAL ASSESSMENT

India developed rapidly in infrastructure, digital systems and geopolitical visibility after 2014.

At the same time, debates surrounding democracy, institutional balance, media freedom and political centralization also intensified.

The future of India depends on balancing development, freedom, security and accountability.
