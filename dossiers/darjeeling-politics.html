THE POLITICAL CHESSBOARD

Darjeeling politics evolved into a constant struggle for territorial influence, identity politics, regional loyalty, party dominance and emotional public mobilization.

Different political organizations claim to represent “the people”, yet many citizens continue questioning whether the hills are genuinely being developed or merely politically controlled.
